About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Plastic Trading

Our Story

Sheeba Enterprise was established in Vapi, one of India's largest industrial areas, with a vision to provide high-quality plastic materials to manufacturing businesses across the country.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for reliability, integrity, and excellence in the plastic trading industry. Our team of experts ensures that every product we supply meets the highest quality standards.

Our Mission

At Sheeba Enterprise, our mission is to be the most trusted supplier of plastic materials in India, providing sustainable solutions that help our customers succeed while minimizing environmental impact.

• Innovation: We continuously seek better ways to serve our customers
